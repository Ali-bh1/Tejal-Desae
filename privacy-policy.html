At Tejal Desae, your privacy is not a formality — it is a reflection of the trust you place in this space. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how it is held.

01

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you engage with this website or its services:

  • Personal Identification: Name, email address, phone number, country, and payment details — when you register, purchase a programme, or submit an application form.
  • Voluntary Information: Information you share during coaching sessions, contact forms, or surveys.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and usage statistics collected via cookies or analytics tools.
02

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to serve you better:

  • To deliver programmes, courses, and services you have purchased
  • To send updates, newsletters, and programme-related communication

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information with third parties.

03

Storage and Protection of Data

Your personal information is stored securely and accessed only by authorised personnel. We use industry-standard encryption and secure servers. As required under the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011, we take reasonable steps to protect your data from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.

04

Sharing of Information

We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

International users acknowledge that their data may be processed outside their country, in compliance with Indian data protection regulations.

05

Cookies and Tracking

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyse trends, and understand how visitors interact with this site. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though some site features may not function as intended.
